Frequently asked questions

What industries do you specialize in?
We serve cost-intensive industries such as Manufacturing, Real Estate & Construction, Financial Services, Healthcare, Technology, and Private Equity portfolio companies.
Do I need a big procurement team to work with you?
No. We can support internal teams or act as your external procurement arm. Our services are scalable and right-sized to your needs.
What kind of savings should I expect?
Most clients realize 8–15% in savings across targeted spend categories. Some projects exceed 20% depending on maturity and contract terms.
How long do engagements take?
Projects range from 4–16 weeks, depending on scope. We move quickly to show results early and often.
Is there a cost to get started?
No. We offer a free spend audit and strategy call to assess your opportunity—risk-free.
How do you measure success?
We define success metrics upfront—typically hard cost savings, process improvements, and ROI. You'll receive regular reports tracking progress.

Our mission is to help companies of all sizes achieve sustainable cost reductions that directly impact their bottom line, without sacrificing quality or operational effectiveness.
